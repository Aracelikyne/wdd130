Half Day Thrills

Experience the excitement of white water rafting on a condensed adventure. Perfect for beginners or those short on time, this trip covers some of the most fun and splashy sections of the river. Our expert guides will ensure a safe and exhilarating experience. Includes all necessary equipment and a riverside snack.

  • Duration: Approximately 3-4 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
  • Highlights: Fun rapids, scenic views, great for families

Full Day River Expedition

Immerse yourself in a full day of white water adventure. This trip takes you through a wider variety of rapids and offers more time to enjoy the stunning river scenery. A delicious riverside lunch is included. Ideal for those looking for a more comprehensive rafting experience.

  • Duration: Approximately 7-8 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging
  • Highlights: Extended time on the river, diverse rapids, riverside lunch

Multi-Day Wilderness Adventure

Embark on an unforgettable multi-day rafting trip through the heart of the wilderness. Camp along the riverbanks, enjoy gourmet meals prepared by our guides, and experience the solitude and beauty of the backcountry. This is the ultimate white water rafting experience for adventure seekers.

  • Duration: 2-3 days (options available)
  • Difficulty: Challenging
  • Highlights: Remote wilderness, camping under the stars, gourmet meals
